Elotes (Mexican Street Corn)

Description

If you don't want elotes, then try this. It's pretty, pretty, pretty good.

This is objectively the best way to eat corn, there are no alternatives that come close.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. elotes Dough
  • 1/2 cup elotes Sauce
  • 6 oz. corn
  • 12 oz. low moisture mayonnaise
  • Pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp. fresh oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and baking sheet to 500 F
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough to 12 inch diameter
  4. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes
  5. Spread thin layer of cheese over dough
  6. Cover with sauce
  7. Spread thin layer of corn, reserving half
  8. Cover with remaining mayonnaise
  9. Spread remaining corn
  10. Using a floured elotes peel, carefully slide constructed elotes onto baking sheet
  11. Bake at 500F for 11 minutes or until crust and cheese are a deep golden brown
