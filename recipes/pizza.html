Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni Pizza is the greatest creation of all time. Delicious crust, cheese, and pepperoni. What else could you want?

No seriously, what else is there that you could possibly want? EAT PIZZA. Be well.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. pizza Dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza Sauce
  • 6 oz. pepperoni
  • 12 oz. low moisture mozzarella
  • Pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp. fresh oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and baking sheet to 500 F
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough to 12 inch diameter
  4. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes
  5. Spread thin layer of cheese over dough
  6. Cover with sauce
  7. Spread thin layer of pepperoni, reserving half
  8. Cover with remaining mozzarella
  9. Spread remaining pepperoni
  10. Using a floured pizza peel, carefully slide constructed pizza onto baking sheet
  11. Bake at 500F for 11 minutes or until crust and cheese are a deep golden brown
